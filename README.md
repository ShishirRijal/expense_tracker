## 💰 Track Your Expenses, Stay in Control!

The **Expense Tracker** is a **Flutter-powered** app that helps you monitor and categorize your daily income and expenses efficiently. 🚀

✨ **Key Highlights:**
- Categorize transactions and add custom categories 🎨
- Data is stored locally using **Hive** for persistence 💾
- Beautiful UI inspired by [Sagar Panchal on Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/SagarPanchal) ✨
- View insightful **transaction charts** 📊
- Edit, delete, and manage transactions effortlessly ✍️
- **Dark mode** support 🌙

## 🚀 Features

### 🎟️ **Money Card**
Displays your **name, income, expenses, and total balance** at the top in a visually appealing card.

### 📂 **Add & Customize Categories**
- Create unlimited **custom categories** 🏷️ with unique **icons and colors**
- Specify whether the category is **income or expense**

### 🛠️ **Edit & Delete Categories**
- Modify category details anytime ✏️
- Delete categories (Warning: All related data will be lost! ⚠️)

### 🖼️ **Profile Photo & Name**
- Personalize the app by adding your **profile picture and name** 📸

### 💾 **Data Persistence**
- Uses **Hive database** to save your data locally, ensuring your transaction history is always secure!

### 📈 **Transaction Charts**
- View your **income and expenses** in an **interactive chart** 📊
- Get **category-wise insights** for better analysis 🔍

### 📜 **Transaction List & Filters**
- View transactions by **day, week, or month**
- Quickly differentiate between **income & expenses**

### ✏️ **Edit Transactions**
- Made a mistake? No worries, edit your transactions anytime ✅

### 📊 **Category-wise Transactions**
- Check **total transactions per category** and track spending habits

### 🌙 **Dark Mode**
- Enjoy a beautifully designed **dark mode** for a seamless experience 😍

### ➕ **Add New Transactions**
- Add **amount, category, date, and notes** with ease
- Delete transactions when needed 🗑️
